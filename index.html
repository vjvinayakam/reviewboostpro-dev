No! ReviewBoostPRO uses the standard wa.me link method. You click a button, WhatsApp opens with the message pre-filled, and you hit send from your own phone. No API needed, no approvals, no fees.
Is asking for reviews against Google's terms?+
No — asking customers for honest reviews is fully allowed by Google. ReviewBoostPRO simply makes it easy for happy customers to leave one. Used by thousands of businesses globally.
How quickly will I see more reviews?+
Most businesses see new Google reviews within 24–48 hours of sending their first requests. On average, a 3–5× increase in reviews within 60 days.
